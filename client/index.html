Night Length Application

This application uses coordinates to find the length of the night on any given day. Night is defined as the period between astronomical twilights, when it is the darkest. In addition, the application calculates the times of sunrise, sunset and the time periods of civil, nautical and astronomical twilights. The calculations are based on a formula from "Almanac for Computers".

Choose a location on the map (or enter coordinates manually in EPSG:4326) and a date
